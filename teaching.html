Teaching

Passionate about making complex ideas accessible. Teaching AI/ML to the next generation of researchers and engineers.

Classroom

Courses Taught

CS 601

Machine Learning

Instructor Autumn 2024

Core graduate course covering supervised learning, optimization, probabilistic models, kernels, ensemble methods, and neural network fundamentals. Includes weekly programming assignments in Python and a semester-long project with real-world datasets.

👥 Enrollment: 112 students · IIT Delhi

CS 701

Deep Learning

Instructor Spring 2024

Advanced graduate course on modern deep learning. Topics include CNNs, RNNs, attention mechanisms, transformers, generative models (GANs, diffusion), and large-scale training techniques. Students reproduce 2 ICLR papers as part of the assessment.

👥 Enrollment: 87 students · IIT Delhi

CS 402

Computer Vision

Teaching Assistant Autumn 2023

Undergraduate elective on classical and modern computer vision. Covers image formation, feature extraction, stereo, optical flow, object recognition, detection, and segmentation. Led weekly lab sessions and office hours.

👥 Enrollment: 145 students · IIT Delhi

CS 302

Data Structures & Algorithms

Teaching Assistant Spring 2023

Core undergraduate course covering arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, sorting, dynamic programming, and algorithm analysis. Designed and graded assignments, conducted weekly problem-solving sessions of 3 hours each.

👥 Enrollment: 200 students · IIT Delhi

CS 501

Pattern Recognition

Teaching Assistant Autumn 2022

Graduate course on statistical pattern recognition, Bayesian classifiers, discriminant analysis, dimensionality reduction (PCA, LDA), HMMs, and SVMs. Developed and maintained course Jupyter notebooks and automated grading scripts.

👥 Enrollment: 65 students · IIT Delhi

CS 801

Advanced NLP

Instructor Spring 2025

PhD-level seminar covering transformer architectures, large language models, RLHF, prompt engineering, retrieval-augmented generation, and multimodal language grounding. Students present recent papers and complete a research project culminating in a workshop submission.

👥 Enrollment: 28 students · IIT Delhi
