Ongoing Projects

Active · 2024–Present Medical AI

MedSight: Multimodal Clinical Report Generation

Developing a vision-language foundation model that generates structured radiology reports from chest X-rays and CT volumes. The system leverages a hierarchical cross-attention mechanism to align visual findings with clinical terminology, achieving state-of-the-art BLEU-4 and CIDEr scores on the MIMIC-CXR and Indiana University datasets. Funded by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship.

Active · 2023–Present Model Efficiency

AdapTok: Adaptive Token Merging for Video Transformers

Investigating dynamic token reduction strategies for video understanding transformers. AdapTok learns to selectively merge spatially and temporally redundant tokens at inference time, reducing FLOPs by up to 65% with less than 1% accuracy drop on Kinetics-400, Something-Something v2, and ActivityNet benchmarks. Accepted at NeurIPS 2025.

Video TransformersToken MergingKinetics-400
Active · 2023–Present Self-Supervised Learning

UniSSL: Unified Self-Supervised Pre-Training for Vision and Language

A unified pre-training framework combining masked image modelling, contrastive multi-view learning, and cross-modal alignment objectives within a single training pipeline. UniSSL produces representations that transfer strongly to both unimodal (ImageNet, CIFAR) and multimodal (VQA, NLVR2) benchmarks, outperforming specialised pre-training schemes by 2–4%.
