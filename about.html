Who I Am

Kuldeep S Yadav

I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, working under the supervision of Prof. Anil Gupta in the Vision & Intelligence Lab. My research focuses on efficient deep learning models for visual perception and cross-modal understanding.

Before my PhD, I completed my B.Tech. and M.Tech. in Computer Science from IIT Roorkee, where I graduated at the top of my class. My master's thesis on self-supervised representation learning received the Best Thesis Award.

Outside research, I contribute to open-source AI tools, mentor undergraduate students, and write about machine learning on my blog. I'm passionate about making AI research accessible and reproducible.
